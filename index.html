assighment 5_Improving visualization of example 2

Improvement (option 1):


The first way to improve the visualisation is to use more rational way to show the data. The goal is to relate all visual factors to quantity.

  1. Fisrt suggestion would be change the "subject, verb, and object" of the data to "who, what, where". As indicated in the issue 3 in the data chapter, some verbs lost subjectivity of frequency due to nature of language.

  2. Use standard bar diagram to illustrate the size of the data rather than circle. The bar diagram of typical Sankey chart has a strong nature of show the connectivity between sectors of data. by doing this, we can see there are much more links from "I" to other data. We immediately have a more clear vision that the data "I" plays a much more strong role in this hypothesis.

  3. The big difference of colors are set between the 3 sectors of datas, in order to point out the key data strategy in this project.

  4. Use a gradient color scheme rather than 2 discrete color as in the orgianal. The saturation of the color link to the size of the data, the big the size, the stronger the saturation will be. By doing this, the data plays more important role in the "happyness" are in brighter colors, which make them more prominent to the viewer.
